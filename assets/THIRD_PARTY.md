# Third-party assets

This project can download free placeholder assets from Kenney or OpenGameArt.

- Kenney UI Pack: https://kenney.nl/assets/ui-pack
- Kenney Topdown Shooter: https://kenney.nl/assets/topdown-shooter
- Quaternius All-in-One (Space ships + asteroids): https://quaternius.com
- Quaternius Ultimate Space Kit (March 2023): https://quaternius.com
- OpenGameArt CC0 SFX:
  - Laser: https://opengameart.org/content/laser (laserthing.wav, laserthing2.wav)
  - Mechanical Explosion: https://opengameart.org/content/mechanical-explosion (mechanical_explosion.wav)
  - Thruster: https://opengameart.org/content/thruster (space_ship.ogg)
  - Click Sound: https://opengameart.org/content/click-sound (sound_click.wav)
